# DESCRIPTION

Get directions from A to B and choose the fastest route based on real-time data.

Plan your trip, check timetables and get live service alerts. Connect your calendar to get automatic trips & leave alerts.

Time poor? Money poor? Environment focused? TripGo gives you transport solutions that fit your priorities.

Works in major cities across the US, Canada, Europe & Australia.


TRANSPORT MODES:

+ Public transport: buses, ferries, subways, trains, trams and more
+ Taxi
+ Shuttle services
+ Car
+ Car Sharing
+ Motorbike
+ Bicycle
+ Bike Sharing
+ Walking

You'll get results for each of those, and also combinations thereof!
